About The Show

Over the Garden Wall is a show about two half-brothers who travel through a mysterious forest to find their way home. Througout their journey, they encounter a variety of strange things. It was released in November of 2014.

The Creator

Patrick McHale created Over the Garden Wall in November 2014. Before working on Over the Garden wall, he worked on an eight-minute animated short called Tome of the Unknown in October 2011, the short then became the basis for Over the Garden Wall.
